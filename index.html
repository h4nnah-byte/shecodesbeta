AI Bias Auditor: Can Your AI Be Fair?

The increasing reliance on AI for critical decisions, such as job candidate screening, risks automating and amplifying historical biases. Our project directly confronts this challenge: Can we truly measure and mitigate bias in AI systems?

What We Explored:

We trained and rigorously audited three machine learning models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and a Fairness-aware model) using real job placement data. Our auditing process involved statistical tests, Disparate Impact Ratio (DIR), and custom fairness metrics.

A Striking Paradox:

Our analysis uncovered a critical paradox: one model, despite attempting to favor women (indicated by a negative male coefficient), unexpectedly still exhibited discriminatory outcomes against them.

Our Core Finding:

High model accuracy does not equate to fairness. Our framework provides a clear methodology to quantify bias, underscoring its absolute necessity before deploying any AI system that shapes people's opportunities. Ensure your AI is not just smart, but equitable.
