About the Project

This project explores common biases in Artificial Intelligence through an interactive, retro-themed experience. Learn about how data, algorithms, and human decisions can influence AI outputs and contribute to unfair or inaccurate results.

Our goal is to raise awareness and provide insights into responsible AI development.
